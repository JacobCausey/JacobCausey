A Little History

Whataburger actually began as a small burger shack in Corpus Christi in 1950! Yes, imagine that, a Texas legend began in 1950 and is still going even stronger than anyone could have imagined when they bought burgers from Harmon Dobson. The company had opened 17 stores by the 1960’s and continued to grow all the way to over 300 restaurants by the 1980’s. Fast forward to 20 years ago in 2001, when Whataburger is a designated a ‘Texas Treasure’ by the 77th Texas Legislature. The burger joint would continue to grow now covering 10 states and leaving people saying, “WHAT A BURGER!” And expansions can continue!
