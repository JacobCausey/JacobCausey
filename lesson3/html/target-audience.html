Overview

Audience

The audience for this site is individuals that are looking for basic weather information including but not limited to: outdoors enthusiast, athletes, families, etc. Their ages can very from 12-100. People should be able to access the site from any device. (Within reason of course) The goal of users seeking to use the site should be looking for basic weather forecasts, information, and warnings.
