White Space and Clean Design

Discord

Discord

In the Discord home page, they do an excellent job with leaving enough white space. Their images and text appear with greater legibility. As well this allows their images to pop out more on the screen.

Visual Hierarchy

Bungie

Bungie.Net

Despite poorly balancing their game's PVP modes, Bungie does an excellent job with Visual Hierarchy. They give you information about the most recent content first, then in smaller boxes below the larger main interest, the place other content. Then any other random links or accessable points that you may not be looking for are placed in smaller less noticable places.

Hick's Law

NHL Shop

NHL Shop

The filter options on this site are dangerous. Mostly because it allows users to narrow down which players they want to look for, which sizes they want, and many other filters that narrow down choices.
